1. Eat a Variety of Foods

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats in your daily meals for balanced nutrition.

2. Control Portion Sizes

Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating. Use smaller plates and listen to your hunger cues.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Limit sugary drinks and excessive caffeine.

4. Limit Processed Foods

Reduce intake of highly processed foods, trans fats, and excess salt or sugar for better health.

5. Plan Your Meals

Prepare healthy meals in advance to avoid last-minute unhealthy choices and maintain a consistent diet.

6. Eat Mindfully

Take time to enjoy your food, chew slowly, and avoid distractions like phones or TV during meals.

7. Consult a Nutritionist

Seek professional advice tailored to your specific dietary needs, health conditions, or fitness goals.
