Overview

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in treating cardiovascular diseases. She specializes in interventional cardiology and preventive cardiology, with a patient-centered approach to care.

Dr. Johnson completed her medical degree at Harvard Medical School and her residency at Massachusetts General Hospital. She is committed to providing compassionate, evidence-based care to all her patients.

Dr. Johnson has been recognized for her excellence in patient care, receiving the "Top Doctor" award from Boston Magazine for five consecutive years. Her research focuses on innovative treatments for coronary artery disease and improving patient outcomes through personalized treatment plans.

Outside of her clinical practice, Dr. Johnson serves as a clinical instructor at Harvard Medical School, where she mentors medical students and residents. She believes in a holistic approach to cardiac care that addresses both physical and emotional health.
