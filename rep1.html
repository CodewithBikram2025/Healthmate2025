Overview

Prof. (Dr.) Bibhore Sengupta is a highly accomplished and senior Pulmonologist, Respiratory & Critical Care Medicine Specialist with more than 30 years of medical experience, including over 23 years of specialization in pulmonary medicine. A former Major in the Indian Army Medical Corps, Dr. Sengupta combines military discipline with world-class clinical expertise in lung health, interventional pulmonology, and respiratory diagnostics.

He is currently associated with leading hospitals and diagnostics centers in Kolkata including Ruby General Hospital, Apollo Clinic (Thakurpukur), Pulse Diagnostics (Behala & Lansdowne), and Desun Hospital. He is also a respected medical educator and academician, having taught medical students and postgraduate trainees for over 17 years as a senior faculty member under WBUHS and other institutions.

Dr. Sengupta is recognized for his expertise in treating chronic respiratory conditions such as COPD, asthma, tuberculosis, ILD, and sleep apnea, as well as in managing respiratory failure, COVID-related lung damage, and lung cancer through advanced bronchoscopic and thoracoscopic techniques.

He is fluent in English, Hindi, and Bengali, allowing him to cater to a broad and diverse patient population.
